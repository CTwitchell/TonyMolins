Super Saiyans

A Super Saiyan is a transformation of legendary and immense proportions of a member of the Saiyan race, when under great emotional and physical stress, in response to a need.

Requirements

  • Be at least 1/4 Saiyan
  • Be face an enemy or force you couldn't possibly beat
  • Be completely drained, out of energy and on the cusp of defeat.
