Super Saiyan

A Super Saiyan is a transformation of legendary and immense proportions of a member of the Saiyan race, when under great emotional and physical stress, in response to a need.

Requirements

  • Be at least 1/4 Saiyan, although half or full is preferable.
  • Be face an enemy or force you couldn't possibly beat
  • Be completely drained, out of energy and on the cusp of defeat.
  • Be extremely angry after something traumatic; i.e. the death of a friend.

Noticeable Changes; Physical

  • The user's hair will become golden, and flare up into a more extreme position.
  • The user will radiate a flame-like golden-yellow aura, possibly causing collateral damage.
  • Muscle mass slightly increases, as well as height.

Noticeable Changes; Psychological

  • Emotions will be in high flux, and likely near uncontrollable.
  • Some negative emotions will be enhanced; i.e. a normally temperate person will become voltaile and vengeful.
  • Most users will find it extremely hard to back down from a fight in this state.

Effects of the transformation

  • Speed, strength, energy projection, and durablility are all increased fiftyfold.
  • Stamina is completely restored.
  • May have negative effects on the enviroment such as, gravitational anomalies, destruction of nearby ground or mountains, or total annihalation of the planet the user is on.
